Welcome to Mrs. Fig's Figgery

Discover our carefully curated collection of healthy, vibrant plants. From elegant figs to spiky pineapples, our variety of green companions give us a reason to smile.

Our Beautiful Plants

Brown Turkey Fig

This is a Brown Turkey Fig tree. It is a bit smaller than the Texas Everbearing but is a beautiful tree. It produces the most fruit of any fig tree we have.

Pineapple Plant

This pineapple plant is more bush than tree, and it has proved quite fruitful with at least one fruit a year for the past few years.

Strawberry Plant

This is a strawberry plant. It may sprout runners to grow into a larger plant. It will produce strawberries in the early summer here in Texas.

Texas Everbearing Fig

This is a Texas Everbearing fig tree. It will grow quite large but is easy to care for and produces multiple batches of fruit.

Celeste Fig

This is a Celeste Fig tree. It seems to be a bit less hardy than the Texas Everbearing but is a beautiful tree.

Fuji Apples

Some of our favorite apples. They will not ripen further and must be pruned to allow the tree to focus on growing.

About Mrs. Fig's Figgery

Welcome to Mrs. Fig's Figgery, where passion for plants meets expert care. Founded with love for all things green, we specialize in providing healthy, thriving plants that bring life and produce yummy fruits.

Our carefully selected collection includes everything from low-maintenance pineapples to statement-making fiddle leaf figs. Each plant is nurtured with care and has dealt with the wild fluctuations of Texas heat and humidity to ice storms in February.

Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just beginning your green journey, we're here to show you how to care for your plants and enjoy the fruits of your labor.
